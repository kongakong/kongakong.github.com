Unable to switch to zsh which is installed from brew

        $ chsh -s `which zsh`
        Changing shell for demo.
        Password for demo:
        chsh: /usr/local/bin/zsh: non-standard shell

Solution: 

Add `/usr/local/bin/zsh` to `/etc/shells`
