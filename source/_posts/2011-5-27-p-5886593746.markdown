My app crashed for no reason.

Turns out it is my mistake: 

{% codeblock lang:objc %}
    [self.view.addSublayer:myLayer];
    [myLayer dealloc];
{% endcodeblock %}

when I mean to do it

{% codeblock lang:objc %}
    [self.view.addSublayer:myLayer];
    [myLayer release];
{% endcodeblock %}
