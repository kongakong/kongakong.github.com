Add two NSNumbers together

You cannot just add one NSNumber to another.

Here is the example of how-to: 

    float res;
    if ([oper isEqualToString:@"+"]) {
        res = [lastNum floatValue] + [curNum floatValue]; 
        return [[NSNumber alloc] initWithFloat:res];
    }
