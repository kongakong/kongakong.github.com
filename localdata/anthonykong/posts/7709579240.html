How to customise your math.sqrt()?

It is an interesting question. Someone posted such as solution: 

# broken into two lines for readability in the blog post
math.sqrt=(lambda sqrt=math.sqrt: lambda x: x.__sqrt__(x)  \
           if hasattr(x,'__sqrt__') else sqrt(x))()
At first glance it is a bit complicated. A simpler solution can be: 
lambda x: x.__sqrt__(x) if hasattr(x,'__sqrt__') else math.sqrt(x)
But the longer version allows you a choice of what sqrt() as default (The lambda sqrt=math.sqrt: part) Source: Python newsgroup
