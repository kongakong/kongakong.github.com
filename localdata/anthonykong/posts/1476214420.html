Statistical Arbitrage

A portfolio of Stocks is constructed by buying previous under-performing stocks, and short-selling previous outperforming stocks by the same dollar amount.

This quantitative equity market-neutral strategy is simple enough to yield analytically tractable expressions for its statistical properties, and realistic enough to illustrate many of the practical challenges of this particular part of the ﬁnancial industry, which has come to be known as “statistical arbitrage” or “statarb”.
